Activities

Nature

Behind the cottage is the mushroom forest, which offers mushroom walks in untouched nature

Across the bay lies a nature reserve with large "giant potholes" shaped during the ice age.

Around the headland Nämndöfjärden stretches out, and a quiet day is a short boat trip to Sandskär - an island next to the lighthouse French stones, warmly recommended. There, picnics, sun and baths can be enjoyed on the south-facing cliffs while the leisure boats pass by.

A boat trip through the canal to Stavsnäs / Vinterhamn is another popular excursion. It is less dependent on calm winds, as the tour goes on mainly protected water.

The waters around Abborrfjället are well suited for fishing for the fishing enthusiast, from both rocks and boats.

Nearby activities

Golf

Abborrfjället is only 7 km from Fågelbro Golf & Country Club with an 18 hole course, 13 km from the ancient Wermdö Gold & Country Club 18 hole course and 24 km from Ingarö Golf Club with its 2 18 hole courses.

Trip to the archipelago

From Stavsnäs Winter Harbor, one can take one of the archipelago boats out to Sandhamn, Möja etc., why not try the Vaxholmsbolaget's North / South line and explore the entire coastline? More information
